# 🌸 Florist Studio

**A florist business diary created by a florist, for florists.**

Florist Studio was created by **Eternal Blooms** to make the everyday admin of running a flower business feel lighter, prettier and easier to manage. It brings pricing, orders, stock, expenses, invoices, customers, content ideas and profit tracking into one calm workspace.

No complicated software. No monthly subscription. No one else handling your data.

---

## ✨ What it helps with

- 💐 Price bouquets and arrangements
- 📋 Save quotes as orders
- 📦 Track stock and low inventory
- 🧾 Create downloadable invoices
- 👥 Manage customers
- 💸 Track expenses
- 📅 See upcoming orders in the calendar
- 📊 View sales, profit and projections
- 💡 Generate content ideas for social media
- 💾 Keep data local to the florist's own device

---

 ## Designed for real florist life

Version 2 introduces the **Petal & Porcelain** interface: sophisticated enough to feel professional, soft enough to feel welcoming, and straightforward enough to use during a busy working day.

It includes three purpose-built layouts:

- **Laptop/desktop:** an elegant sidebar workspace with roomy dashboards
- **iPad/tablet:** a centred two-row command bar and touch-friendly columns
- **Phone:** a compact two-row bottom dock designed for one-handed use

---

## 🌷 Why I made this

I wanted something that felt useful for real florists — not cold, corporate or overly technical. Florists need tools that work quickly on a phone, look beautiful, and help us understand our numbers without needing to be accountants.

Florist Studio is still growing, but the goal is simple:

> Help florists spend less time stressing over admin and more time creating beautiful flowers.

---

## 🔒 Data

Florist Studio is local-first. It does not collect customer, order or business data for Eternal Blooms. Each florist keeps control of their own information using browser storage and local backup/data files.

Regular backups are still recommended.
