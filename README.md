# 🌸 Florist Studio

**A pretty, local-first business diary for florists — created by Eternal Blooms.**

Florist Studio was born from a very real florist problem: prices in one note, orders in another, stock in your head, receipts in a drawer, and profit calculations happening far too late at night.

This project brings the everyday running of a small flower business into one gentle workspace: bouquet pricing, orders, stock, customers, expenses, invoices, calendar planning, content ideas and profit projections.

It is made for florists who want something useful, calm and beautiful — not intimidating business software.

---

## ✨ What Florist Studio does

- 💐 Price bouquets with flowers, stock, labour, markup, fees and delivery
- 📋 Save calculator quotes straight into orders
- 📦 Connect orders to inventory so stock usage is tracked
- 👥 Save customers, preferences and notes
- 🧾 Create branded invoices using the florist’s own business name/logo
- 💰 Track expenses
- 📅 View upcoming and completed orders
- 📊 See sales, expenses, profit and projections
- 💡 Generate content ideas for Instagram and marketing
- 💾 Keep data local to each florist’s own device/browser
- 📱 Work on mobile and desktop through GitHub Pages

---

## 🔒 Local-first privacy

Florist Studio does **not** collect everyone’s business data into one shared database.

Each florist keeps their own data on their own device/browser and can export backups. This makes it better suited for independent florists who want control over their customer, order and stock information.

Please make regular backups. Browser storage can be lost if site data is cleared.
